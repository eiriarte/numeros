Apocope and gender

The numerals uno and veintiuno become un and veintiún, respectively, when they stand before a masculine noun, adjective or another numeral (like mil):

OneUno
One dogUn perro
One beautiful dogUn bello perro
Twenty-oneVeintiuno
Twenty-one dollarsVeintiún dólares
Twenty-one thousandVeintiún mil

The numerals uno and veintiuno have also feminine forms: una and veintiuna. These are needed when the number precedes a feminine noun or adjective:

One (ball)Una (pelota)
One small ballUna pequeña pelota
Twenty-one poundsVeintiuna libras
Twenty-one thousand poundsVeintiuna mil libras
