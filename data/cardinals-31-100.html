The numbers from 31 all the way to 99 are formed simply by writing the tens and the units, separated by the word y (and).

31treinta y uno
32treinta y dos
33treinta y tres
……
97noventa y siete
98noventa y ocho
99noventa y nueve

Apocope and gender

The numerals treinta y uno, cuarenta y uno, etc., become treinta y un, cuarenta y un, and so on, when they stand before a masculine noun, adjective or another numeral (like mil):

Thirty-oneTreinta y uno
Thirty-one dollarsTreinta y un dólares
Thirty-one thousandTreinta y un mil

These numerals have also feminine forms: treinta y una, cuarenta y una, etc. These are needed when the number precedes a feminine noun or adjective:

Thirty-one poundsTreinta y una libras
Thirty-one thousand poundsTreinta y una mil libras
